Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to X2 Blocks Premium. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect and process your information when you play our game.

2. Information We Collect

We collect certain data to provide a better gaming experience and manage global rankings:

  • Personal Information: Name and Profile Picture (Only if you sign in with Google).
  • Identifiers: Email address and unique User IDs for account syncing.
  • Game Data: Scores, total merges, and level progress.
  • Device Data: OS version, device model, and Country (via IP).

3. Account and Data Deletion

Google Play requires us to provide a method for users to request account and data deletion. You have full control over your data:

A. Direct In-App Deletion (Recommended)

You can permanently delete your entire account, including scores, diamonds, and profile, directly within the game. Go to Settings > Account & Data > Delete All Data and confirm. This process is instant and cannot be undone.

B. Request via Email

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by contacting us at resulkilicaslan@gmail.com. Please provide your User ID or the email address associated with your Google Sign-In. We will process your request and remove all your data from our servers within 7 business days.

4. Ads and Third Parties

We use Google AdMob to show advertisements. Our partners may use device identifiers to personalize ads based on your interests.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at: resulkilicaslan@gmail.com
